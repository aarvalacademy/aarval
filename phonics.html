About the Program

About Aarval Fun Phonics

Aarval Fun Phonics is a structured early reading program designed to build strong foundational skills in every child.

We focus on teaching pure letter sounds and systematic left-to-right blending, helping children read confidently and independently the right way, from the very beginning.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🌟 Our Vision

To build strong, independent readers with correct pronunciation and confident English skills from an early age.

🎯 Our Mission

To deliver a structured phonics program using pure letter sounds and systematic left-to-right blending, ensuring every child develops accurate reading skills and a strong foundation.

How We Teach

The Aarval Phonics Approach

Three key pillars that make our method work.

🔤

Pure Sound Teaching

We teach clear, standalone letter sounds. No 'buh-luh' method children learn the correct pure sound from day one.

➡️

Left-to-Right Blending

Children read sound-by-sound from left to right not guessing by 2-letter families. This builds accurate, confident reading.

📚

Strong Reading Foundation

We ensure strong basics before moving to advanced words no shortcuts, no skipping steps.
