About the Program

Aarval Little Arts

Little Arts is a creative art program at Aarval Academy where children explore the joy of drawing, painting, and crafting in a warm, encouraging environment.

We believe every child is an artist. Our program helps them discover that building confidence, creativity, and a lifelong love for artistic expression.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🌟 Our Vision

To inspire young minds to explore creativity and imagination through art, helping children develop confidence, curiosity, and a lifelong love for artistic expression.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide engaging, structured art classes where children learn fundamental techniques, experiment with colours and materials, and express their ideas freely.

  • Creative thinkers
  • Confident self-expression
  • Strong artistic foundation
  • Joyful learning through art
