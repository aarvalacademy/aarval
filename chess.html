About the Program

Chess Is More Than
a Game at Aarval

At Aarval Academy, chess is a powerful tool to build thinking minds. We nurture young learners with patience, precision, and purpose helping every child grow at their own pace.

Our coaches focus on understanding over memorization and long-term growth over shortcuts. Every session is a step towards becoming a stronger, more confident thinker.
