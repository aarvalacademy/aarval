Which Level is My Child?

Just Starting Out
Age 4 – 6 · Never touched a chess piece
  • Learn the name and moves of each piece
  • Simple checkmates step by step
  • Short 30-min sessions — right for young attention spans
  • Parent can sit with the child during class
Most Popular
Knows the Basics, Ready to Grow
Age 6 – 12 · Knows how pieces move
  • Learn to plan 2–3 moves ahead
  • Spot threats before they happen
  • Weekly puzzles to sharpen thinking
  • Friendly club matches for experience
Serious About Competing
Age 10 – 14 · Wants to win tournaments
  • Deep strategy and opening preparation
  • Study real grandmaster games
  • Full tournament preparation support
  • Rating-focused structured training
